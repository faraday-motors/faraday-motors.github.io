Electric Vehicle Marketing

Overview

Background and problem definition

The Company

  • Electric Car startup in Detroit
  • Billionaire founder with multiple investment sources
  • Successful prototypes and racing models
  • Revving up factories and production in the next six months

Marketing Department

Has a limited budget and wants to maximize the return on advertising spending. Competing with bigger brands with more revenue and historuc data, wishes to market efficiently to the markets with the most potential and potential leads with the highest likelihood of buying an electric car.

Problem Statement

Using public and open data sets:

  • identify the best target demographics and markets for marketing and broadcast advertising
  • rate each potential lead based on likelihood of buying an electric car
  • build tools that help marketing campaign managers to make informed data driven decision
