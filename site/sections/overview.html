Framing the problem, who we're solving it for, and its impact

Who Are We

We are data scientists at Faraday Motors, an up-and-coming electric car startup that is revving up production of our first vehicle.

What's the Problem

Our marketing team has a limited budget and wishes to make data driven decisions to find optimal markets and leads.

What's the Impact

Our company's success is highly dependent on how many people buy our cars, thus we need a high ROI on advertising in order to succeed.
