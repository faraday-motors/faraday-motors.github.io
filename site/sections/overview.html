Overview

Framing the problem, who we're solving it for, and its impact

Who Are We

Faraday Motors is an up-and-coming electric car startup that is revving up production of our first vehicle. We are data scientists trying to help all aspects of business be driven by information and wisdom gleaned from data.

What's the Problem

Without any prior sales data, our marketing team has no historic information to base marketing strategies on. Additionally, constrained by a limited budget, they wish to make eficient and effective data driven decisions to find optimal markets and leads.

What's the Impact

Electric cars are projected to overtake gasoline cars some time in the future. Entrenched car makers are only starting to play in the space. We have the first mover advantage and capitalizing on that will help us survive, compete and grow against the big players.
